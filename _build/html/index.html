文档是如何组成¶

Django 有丰富的文档. A high-level overview of how it’s organized will help you know where to look for certain things:

  • 教程 take you by the hand through a series of steps to create a Web application. Start here if you’re new to Django or Web application development. Also look at the “快速入门” below.

  • 专题指南 discuss key topics and concepts at a fairly high level and provide useful background information and explanation.

  • 参考指南 contain technical reference for APIs and other aspects of Django’s machinery. They describe how it works and how to use it but assume that you have a basic understanding of key concepts.

  • How-to 指南 are recipes. They guide you through the steps involved in addressing key problems and use-cases. They are more advanced than tutorials and assume some knowledge of how Django works.
